Smash Remix mod created by halofactory

DISCLAIMER: I do not guarantee that this mod will work with other Smash Remix mods, nor future versions of Smash Remix. Please see https://github.com/Gregery94/Smash-Remix-Skin-Change for latest version.
-- -- -- -- -- -- -- -- -- -- -- -- -- 

requirements:
 - A ROM of SMASH REMIX 0.9.7.

steps:
 - use xdelta to apply the patch to your SMASH REMIX 0.9.7 output file.

changes:
 - Lucas's green skin is replaced with his ultimate skin with Boney's face and brown shorts.
 - Young Link's white skin is pink.
